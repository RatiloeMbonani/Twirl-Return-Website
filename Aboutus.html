ABOUT US

At Twirls & Rentals we believe every young woman deserves to feel beautiful, confident and unforgattable on her matric dance night. Our journing began with reusing stunning dresses, they should not catch dust in the closets but shine again. By reusing pre-loved gowns, we make luxury fashon affordable, accessible and sustainabl.

Every gown has a story and now it's ready to be part of yours.

WHY RENT?

  • Affordable elegance without spending alot of money.
  • Reduce wasting and give gowns a longer life.
  • Our collecton is very unique, featuring gowns that are hard to find.
  • No need to worry about storing or reselling after the event.

BENEFITS OF RENTING WITH US

  • Professional cleaning included after every rental.
  • Size options tailored for different body types
  • Wide range of designer and unique gowns
